Tower Defense

In this project which I have called Tower Defense, we were ordered to make — you guessed it — a tower defense game. As you can see in the documentation, I did not spend a whole lot of time programming in this project. That is due to me wanting to spend time experimenting with art implementation and art creation. So in terms of programming, it really is the minimum for the assignment. However, every single sprite and art piece (except the skeleton enemy) was created by me. When talking about some of the individual features, I’ll explain the targeting of the towers using a visual sheet.
